About Us

Who We Are

Terramax Academy was founded with a single belief every student deserves access to expert teaching, modern tools and an environment that pushes them to grow. Located in Latifabad, Hyderabad, we serve learners from Nursery level all the way to professional IT certifications.

From STEM equipped computer labs to structured coaching for matric and intermediate students our classrooms are built to ignite curiosity and develop real skills that open real doors.
